**Click the ◀ (Previous) button ONE time** to go back to **Kartik 2083**

## Why This Happens

The BS calendar months don't align exactly with AD calendar months. 

- **November 1-15, 2026** = **Kartik 17-30, 2083** (has festivals!)
- **November 16-30, 2026** = **Mangsir 1-15, 2083** (quiet period)

When you view "Mangsir 2083 / Nov-Dec", you're seeing the END of November (which is quiet) plus early December.

## Solution

**Click the ◀ Previous button** to go from **Mangsir** back to **Kartik**, and you'll see all the festivals! 🎊

3. **Restart server**: Already done with `--force` flag
